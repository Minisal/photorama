About Me

Research Interest

My research interests are in the fields of computer vision and computer graphics, especially modeling scene information with neural implicit functions. I'm also very interested in special effects in game development, especially lighting and materials. Currently, my focus is on monocular 3D reconstruction/fusion and neural expression and rendering. Computer Graphics (Neural Rendering) Computer Vision (3D Reconstruction) Machine Learning
